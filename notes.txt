JSX = JavaScript + XML

HTML
    <div id="message">
        <h2>From: John Doe</h2>
        <h2>To: Jane Doe</h2>
        <p>Hello, how are you?</p>
    </div>

XML
    <message>
        <from>John Doe</from>
        <to>Jane Doe</to>
        <message>Hello, how are you?</message>
    </message>
